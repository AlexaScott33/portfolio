ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Alexa.

I'm a full stack developer, born and raised in New York and currently living in California. I love developing new apps and working with a team of programmers who help to do so.

I enjoy solving problems and learning new technologies, languages, tips, and tricks. I frequently use React, Redux, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB when building.
